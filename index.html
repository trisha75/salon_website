Salon Policy & FAQs

Q: Should I book an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but booking in advance helps ensure you get a slot right away without waiting.

Q: What if I am running late?

We hold appointments for 10 minutes. If you are later than that, we will try to fit you into the next open walk-in spot.

Q: Do you accept UPI digital payments?

Yes, we fully accept GooglePay, PhonePe, Paytm, and cash payments at the counter.
